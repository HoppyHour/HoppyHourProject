Our effort today is to provide the students and faculty of the UCSD Extension Data Sciences and Visualization program an opportunity to view our visualizations from various data sets (data sources provided from Craft Beer in San Diego https://craftbeerinsandiego.com and the following web resources). All source data and visualizations are provided for reference.

Our analysis was to visualize and understand Craft Brewing and its increase in popularity. We used Python/Pandas with Matplotlib components to plot the data. Mapping technology to show brewery data across the United States and the San Diego Craft Breweries.



Our objective is to build a series of visualizations to showcase the following:

  • San Diego Craft Breweries (table data with JavaScript sorting library)
  • San Diego Craft Breweries / Taproom locations (geo mapping and leaflty ploting)
  • United States Craft Brewing vs United States Brew Consumption
