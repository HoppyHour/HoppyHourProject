Beer Style Analysis

The data for this graph was pulled from Beer Advocate and helps to visualize the popularity of different beers categorically. The largest 3 types of beer categories are lagers, ales (American pale ales and India pale ales making up the biggest chunk of popular ales) and stouts.

The outer ring shows the amount of beers in these categories and the inner ring shows these beers further separated into more specific categories to detail further popularity composition.

About half the graph is still composed of beers aside from the most popular categories, which leads us to believe that the most popular types of craft beers are not necessarily ales, stouts nor lagers, but rather uniquely crafted brews which pique the interest of craft beer enthusiasts.

5 Largest Categories of Beer

  • Stouts
  • Pale Ales
  • Other Ales
  • Lagers
  • Other Beers
